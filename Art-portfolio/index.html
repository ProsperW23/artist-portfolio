About

Museum-grade, but human.

My practice is editorial: each piece is composed with the discipline of print. I treat whitespace as material and hierarchy as a form of respect. Whether a cover illustration or a conceptual plate, the goal is the same: to create a quiet object of attention — museum-grade, unhurried, exact.
